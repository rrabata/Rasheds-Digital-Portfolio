AI Lab Artifact: Future of Jordan

This artifact documents an AI Lab exercise that used generative AI to create a future-oriented visual concept of Jordan while preserving recognizable cultural references. The portfolio version presents the finished image, the prompt, and an academic brief as a complete case-study artifact.

TitleAI Lab: Future of Jordan
ObjectiveCreate a future-facing image of Jordan that still reflects place, culture, and recognizable national identity.
Value PropositionShows prompt design, visual communication, cultural specificity, and the ability to convert AI Lab work into a polished public artifact.
RelevanceRelevant to instructors, employers, and collaborators who want evidence of emerging AI literacy and audience-aware documentation.
Description The artifact packages the completed image, the original prompt, and a concise written explanation into a downloadable brief that demonstrates how a lab activity can become a professional resource.
Unique Value Combines generative image ideation with culturally grounded prompt design instead of relying on a generic futuristic image prompt.
Process
  • Defined the visual goal as a future-oriented image of Jordan.
  • Specified Petra, Jordanian people, and Arabic calligraphy to anchor the output culturally.
  • Generated the image in ChatGPT using a detailed prompt and reviewed the result for cultural and visual alignment.
  • Converted the exercise into a portfolio artifact with a downloadable brief and public-facing explanation.
