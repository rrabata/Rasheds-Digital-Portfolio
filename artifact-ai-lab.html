TitleAI Lab: Future of Jordan
IntroductionThis artifact demonstrates how prompt design can direct a generative AI system toward a culturally grounded visual result instead of a generic futuristic scene.
DescriptionThe artifact includes the completed image, the original prompt, and a concise write-up that explains how the visual was planned, generated, and documented for a professional portfolio audience.
ObjectiveCreate a future-oriented image of Jordan that preserves recognizable cultural and geographic references, including Petra, Arabic calligraphy, and people represented in traditional dress.
Process
  • Defined the visual goal as a future-oriented image of Jordan with clear cultural anchors.
  • Specified Petra, Jordanian people, and Arabic calligraphy in the prompt to anchor the output culturally.
  • Generated the image in ChatGPT using a detailed natural-language prompt and reviewed the output for alignment with the intended setting and symbols.
  • Packaged the result into a portfolio artifact with the final image, a downloadable brief, and assignment-aligned documentation.

Prompt Used
“Generate an image that showcases Jordan, the country in the future. Make sure you include the traditional aspects that make Jordan unique, such as Petra (one of the 7 wonders of the world), make sure you include people in the image to show the viewer what Jordanians look like. Having Arabic calligraphy somewhere would be nice as well.”
